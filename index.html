Introduction:

For my project I chose to interview my two boys and have them teach me how to do something they enjoy. Owen is my introvert and I have to work to get him out of his shell, but he really enjoys basketball. So he chose to talk about how to do a basketball move. Peyton, on the other hand, is outgoing and will talk about just about anything. He is on a rock climbing team and decided to talk about how to do a dynamic move in rock climbing where you jump off the wall from one hold to another.

Peyton: How to do a dyno while rock clmibing

  1. Get your feet as high as possible given the footholds.
  2. Crouch down or to the side, loading up your muscles and focus your eyes on the target hold
  3. Explode up through your toes, while pulling yourself back into the wall with your arms
  4. Grab the hold you are jumping to with the proper grip given the hold type
